# TASK-001 — Technical Audit of MusicDNA

## Objective

Perform a complete technical audit of the MusicDNA repository.

Do NOT add new features.

Do NOT redesign the architecture.

The goal is to evaluate the current implementation.

## Inspect every analysis engine.

For each engine report:

- Purpose
- Current status
- Does it work correctly?
- Known limitations
- Confidence level
- Suggested improvements
- Priority

## Verify especially:

- Energy
- BPM
- Harmony
- Melody
- Pitch
- Spectrum
- Structure
- Emotion
- Production
- Discovery
- Statistics
- Knowledge

## Deliverables

Create:

AUDIT_REPORT.md

The report must contain:

- Executive summary
- Engine-by-engine review
- Critical bugs
- Technical debt
- Quick wins
- Recommended order of implementation

Do not modify production code.

Audit only.
